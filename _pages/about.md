Hi, I'm **Chris (Haolin) Qu**, a full-stack and backend engineer with 2+ years of experience designing and scaling distributed systems and data-driven applications.

I specialize in building microservices with **Spring Boot** and **RESTful APIs**, optimizing performance with **Redis** and asynchronous processing, and deploying resilient solutions on **AWS** and **Kubernetes**. My work spans real-time data pipelines and high-traffic transactional systems, with a strong focus on data reliability, system scalability, and secure service integration.

Currently at [Daxwell](https://www.daxwell.com), I architect and scale a distributed ERP/CRM platform on AWS EKS, owning end-to-end design across microservices, data pipelines, and infrastructure.

I hold an **M.S. in Computer Science** from [Washington University in St. Louis](https://wustl.edu) and a **B.E. in Software Engineering** from [Southwest University](https://www.swu.edu.cn).

Outside of work, I enjoy basketball, tennis, Texas Hold'em, and fitness.
